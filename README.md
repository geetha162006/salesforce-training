# Day 1 - CRM Basics

 What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer data, interactions, sales, and business relationships in one place.

It helps businesses:
- Improve customer relationships
- Track sales activities
- Manage leads and opportunities
- Increase productivity and revenue

---

 Why Companies Use Salesforce?

Salesforce is one of the most popular CRM platforms used by companies because it provides:

- Cloud-based access
- Easy customer data management
- Sales automation
- Reports and dashboards
- Workflow automation
- Scalability for businesses
- Integration with multiple applications

Companies use Salesforce to improve customer engagement and business efficiency.

---
 Salesforce Objects
 1. Account
An Account represents a company or organization with which the business has a relationship.

Example:
- Infosys
- TCS
- Amazon

---
 2. Contact
A Contact represents a person associated with an account.

Example:
- Employee name
- Customer representative
- Manager details

---
 3. Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential sales deal or business opportunity.

It contains:
- Deal amount
- Stage
- Close date
- Probability of winning the deal

Example:
A company planning to purchase software services.

---

Real-World Mapping
